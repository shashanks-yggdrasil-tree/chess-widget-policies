# Privacy Policy for Chess Widget – Fianchetto

Effective date: April 25, 2026

Chess Widget – Fianchetto ("the app") is a widget-first Android app. This policy explains what data the app processes and why. The app is offered in a free, ad-supported version and a paid version; differences are noted below.

## Data We Process

1. Lichess OAuth access token, only when you choose to connect your Lichess account.
2. Local app preferences and widget state (board theme, mode, puzzle progress, and related settings).
3. Tournament source URLs or other configuration values entered by you.
4. Lichess account metadata returned by API calls you trigger (for example username, ratings, and ongoing game identifiers).
5. Device or other identifiers (for example, the Android Advertising ID and a crash-reporting installation identifier), as described below.

## Why We Process Data

1. To authenticate your optional connected Lichess account.
2. To fetch puzzle, game, tournament, and account data requested by you from Lichess APIs.
3. To render and persist widget state on your device.
4. To show ads in the free version, and to diagnose crashes and improve stability.

## Advertising

1. The free version uses Google AdMob to display ads. The paid version does not include ads.
2. AdMob may collect and share a device identifier (the Android Advertising ID) with Google to display and measure ads, limit ad frequency, and detect invalid or fraudulent traffic.
3. You can reset or delete your Advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads on most Android devices).
4. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Crash Diagnostics

1. The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash and stability data, including a Firebase installation identifier, device and OS information, and crash diagnostics.
2. This data is used only to monitor app health and fix bugs.

## Data Sharing

1. Requests are sent to Lichess API endpoints needed for selected features.
2. In the free version, a device identifier is shared with Google (AdMob) for advertising and related measurement and fraud-prevention purposes.
3. Crash diagnostics are processed by Google (Firebase Crashlytics).
4. We do not sell personal data.
5. The app does not operate a backend server that stores your account data.

## Storage and Retention

1. Most settings and state are stored locally on your device.
2. OAuth data remains until you disconnect your account, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

## Security

1. Network requests use HTTPS.
2. Sensitive session/token storage uses encrypted shared preferences when available on the device.

## Children

This app is not directed to children under 13.
